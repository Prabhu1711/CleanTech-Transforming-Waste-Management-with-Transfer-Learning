Scenario 1: Automated Diagnostic Systems
Healthcare Automation

Integrating HematoVision into automated diagnostic systems in clinical settings revolutionizes blood analysis by capturing images of blood samples, classifying cells in real-time, and generating detailed reports.

Key Benefits:
  • Reduces manual workload on pathologists
  • Speeds up diagnostic processes
  • Ensures high accuracy in results
  • Improves patient care and treatment efficiency
Processing time: < 2 seconds per image
Scenario 2: Remote Medical Consultations
Telemedicine Enhancement

HematoVision enhances telemedicine platforms by allowing healthcare providers to upload blood cell images for automated analysis, enabling timely and accurate assessments without in-person visits.

Key Benefits:
  • Enables remote diagnostic capabilities
  • Improves access to specialized medical expertise
  • Serves remote and underserved areas
  • Reduces travel time and costs for patients
Accessible from anywhere with internet
Scenario 3: Educational Tools
Medical Training

HematoVision's classification model integrates into educational tools for medical training, allowing students and laboratory technicians to upload and analyze blood cell images with instant feedback.

Key Benefits:
  • Hands-on learning experience
  • Instant feedback and validation
  • Enhanced understanding of cell morphology
  • Practical skills development
Suitable for students and professionals
